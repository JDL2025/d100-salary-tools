Current D100 Insurance Snapshot

1
D100's Family Plan Pool Each year, the Board sets aside $150,000 — a fixed pot of money dedicated to helping teachers afford family-level health coverage. This applies to Employee+Spouse, Employee+Children, and Family tiers across all plans.
2
Divided Equally… For Better or Worse That $150,000 is split equally among every teacher enrolled in any coverage tier above single. More enrollees = smaller share per person.
3
The Problem With 53 teachers on non-single coverage tiers in 2025–26, each teacher receives only $235.85/month ($2,830/year). More enrollees = smaller share per person.
